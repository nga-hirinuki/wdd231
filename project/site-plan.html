Individual Project Site Plan

Site name: Tutu – Graphic & Website Development Services

This name representing how a client brings an issue or problem that needs to be looked at, adjusted, or fixed.

Site Purpose:

The website’s purpose is to show the graphic design and web development services offered, display sample projects, and provide a simple form for clients to request help.

Scenarios

“My website isn’t displaying correctly on mobile. Can FixWeb4Fee help me fix it?” A visitor wants quick help with a common website problem.

“I need a simple logo and clean layout for my new business. What design services do you offer?” A visitor is looking for basic graphic design support.Tutu – Graphic & Website Development Services
