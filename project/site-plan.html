Individual Project Site Plan

Site name: NGATOKHIR – Graphic Design & Website Development subscription

The business name is named after me. Taking the first three letters of my name

Site Purpose:

The purpose of this website is to offer businesses an easy, affordable way to access professional web development and graphic design through simple monthly subscriptions. Instead of hiring multiple freelancers or paying large one-off project fees, clients can choose a subscription package that gives them ongoing support, consistent updates, and high-quality design and web services every month.

Scenarios

“Scenario 1: The Busy Small Business Owner

“I already have a website, but it’s outdated and I don’t have time to fix it. I also constantly need new graphics for social media and promotions. Can I subscribe to a plan where you handle both every month?”

Scenario 2: The Growing Brand Needing Everything Done

“We’re growing fast and need ongoing support — new pages, landing pages, monthly maintenance, plus logos, banners, and marketing graphics. Do you have a package that covers both design and web so we don’t have to hire multiple freelancers?”
